The Design Practice of Emilie Egan

I'm a human who is passionate about creating innovative and effective solutions that solve real-world problems.

My background in international business, fashion, and visual design gives me a unique perspective and allows me to approach design challenges from multiple angles.

One of the core principles that drives my work is human-centered design. I believe that every design solution should prioritize the needs and experiences of users, and that by doing so, we can create products that are not only functional and effective, but also meaningful and enjoyable to use.
